# Third-Party Data and Acknowledgments

## Protein Data Bank (PDB) Structures
Protein structure files in `data/` are sourced from the RCSB Protein 
Data Bank (https://www.rcsb.org). PDB data is released under CC0 1.0 
Universal (Public Domain Dedication).

Citation: H.M. Berman, J. Westbrook, Z. Feng, G. Gilliland, T.N. Bhat, 
H. Weissig, I.N. Shindyalov, P.E. Bourne. The Protein Data Bank. 
Nucleic Acids Research, 28: 235-242 (2000).

## AlphaFold Protein Structure Database
Validation analyses reference structures from the AlphaFold Protein 
Structure Database (https://alphafold.ebi.ac.uk), made available under 
CC-BY 4.0.

Citation: Jumper, J. et al. Highly accurate protein structure prediction 
with AlphaFold. Nature 596, 583-589 (2021).

## Ramachandran Backbone Statistics
Superpotential calibration uses dihedral angle statistics derived from 
the Top8000 curated dataset (Richardson Lab, Duke University).
